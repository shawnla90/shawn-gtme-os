## Tweet 3 ‚Äî Problem

## Tweet 4 ‚Äî Build

built a skill: agent-context-handoff.

you say full scope of this chat or handoff to new thread. it produces a markdown doc:
- partner and context
- what we accomplished
- key files (real paths)
- open questions
- next steps
- workflow hooks

## Tweet 5 ‚Äî Results
