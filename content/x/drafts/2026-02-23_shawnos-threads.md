## Thread 3: Anti-Lead Magnet Thesis (5 tweets)

**Tweet 1 (Hook)**:
everyone's downloading skill packs that were already stale the day they shipped.

I stopped packaging knowledge into downloads. I built a living website instead. 🧵

---

**Tweet 2**:
the problem with lead magnets:

they freeze one moment in time. a PDF from January doesn't know your tools updated in February. a download from last month doesn't reflect the workflow you rebuilt this week.

snapshots pretending to be systems.

---

**Tweet 3**:
so I built the opposite.

shawnos.ai is a living site. it updates when I learn something. when a tool changes. when I find a better pattern.
