# Tamagotchi Teaser Campaign

## Campaign: "Feed Your AI"

**Objective**: Build anticipation for the evolution system before V3 ships
**Timing**: Can start NOW, during product build phase
**Duration**: Ongoing until V3 launch

---

## Concept

Drip-feed the evolution system one piece at a time. Each post reveals a new layer. By launch day, the audience already understands the mechanic and wants to try it.
