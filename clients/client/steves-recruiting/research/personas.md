# SteveS Personas — Who He Calls

## Primary Contact Types

Steve cold calls the people who make hiring decisions for technical roles.

### Hiring Managers / Engineering Leads
- **Titles**: Engineering Manager, Director of Engineering, VP of Engineering, Tech Lead, Development Manager
- **Why**: Direct authority over technical hiring, know the role requirements
- **Approach**: Peer-to-peer, "I have candidates who match what you're looking for"

### HR / Talent Acquisition
- **Titles**: HR Director, Talent Acquisition Manager, Recruiting Manager, Head of HR, HR Business Partner
- **Why**: Own the hiring process, manage vendor relationships with recruiters
- **Approach**: Professional services pitch, "I can fill this role faster"

### IT Leadership
- **Titles**: IT Director, IT Manager, CIO, VP of IT, Director of Technology
- **Why**: Own IT hiring decisions, especially infrastructure and support roles
- **Approach**: Similar to engineering leads, role-specific

### C-Suite (Smaller Companies)
- **Titles**: CEO, CTO, COO, Founder
- **Why**: At smaller companies, the exec IS the hiring decision-maker
- **Approach**: Direct, time-conscious, lead with candidate quality

- Steve calls on direct lines, so mobile number is the priority enrichment
