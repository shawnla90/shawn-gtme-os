# SteveS ICP

## Target Companies

- **Type**: Companies actively hiring for technical roles
- **Geography**: Ohio (primary), Great Lakes region (Michigan, Minnesota)
- **Signal**: Open technical job postings — this is the primary qualification signal
- **Size**: TBD — Steve works across company sizes as long as they're hiring

## Target Roles Being Filled

Steve recruits for these types of positions:
- Software Engineers
- IT professionals
- Technical leaders / engineering managers
- Other technical roles in the Ohio region

## The Two-Sided Model

Steve doesn't just find companies — he matches from both sides:

**Lead side (companies)**: Companies in Ohio with open technical roles
**Candidate side (talent)**: Steve's existing network of qualified technical candidates

The qualification isn't just "are they hiring" — it's "are they hiring for roles I have candidates for."

## Qualification Criteria

A qualified lead for Steve meets:
- [ ] Company is in Ohio (or Great Lakes region)
- [ ] Actively hiring for technical roles (job postings live)
- [ ] Role matches Steve's candidate pool capabilities
- [ ] Has identifiable hiring contact (name + phone/email)
- [ ] Not already in Steve's existing pipeline
