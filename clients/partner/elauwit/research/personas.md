# Elauwit Buyer Personas

## Persona Tiers

### Tier 1: Economic Buyer

- **Titles**: Owner, Managing Partner, Principal
- **Focus**: Cash flow, NOI impact
- **Messaging**: Financial frame — $0 CapEx, revenue impact, cost elimination
- **Campaign Approach**: Executive Thread play, authority-bias positioning

### Tier 2: Operational Buyer

- **Titles**: VP of Operations, VP of Technology, Asset Manager
- **Focus**: Operational efficiency, resident experience, infrastructure reliability
- **Messaging**: Operational frame — eliminate complaints, future-proof, reduce maintenance
- **Campaign Approach**: Gatekeeper Navigation play, role-based referral asks

## Clay/Claygent Job Title Queries

### NaaS Persona Group (Property Management + IT/VP Ops)

**Bracketed (all-in-one query)**:
```
[Property Manager, Regional Property Manager, VP of Operations, VP of Technology, Director of Operations, Director of IT, IT Manager, Regional Manager, Portfolio Manager, VP of Property Management]
```

**Separated by function**:

Manager-level:
```
Property Manager, Regional Property Manager, Regional Manager, Portfolio Manager, IT Manager
```

VP/Director-level:
```
VP of Operations, VP of Technology, Director of Operations, Director of IT, VP of Property Management
```

## Query Usage Notes

- Use **bracketed format** for Claygent "find people" enrichments where you want all personas in one pass
- Use **separated format** for segmented campaigns or when building persona-specific sequences
- NaaS personas map to the "Quote the Switch" campaign pattern
- Managed WiFi personas map to development-focused messaging
- Always cross-reference against portfolio size (1,000–20,000 units) to avoid unqualified targets
