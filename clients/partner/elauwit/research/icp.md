# Elauwit ICP

## Target Accounts (Hybrid Approach)

- **Company Type**: Privately held Multifamily Owner-Operators & Developers
- **Portfolio Size**: 1,000 – 20,000 total units
- **Geography**: Nationwide capability
  - **2026 Field Marketing Focus**: DMV, Southeast (FL, GA, Carolinas), Texas
- **Unit Count Sweet Spot**: 200+ units per property (unit economics threshold)

## Asset Profiles & Product Fit

### Primary Target: Class B / Vintage 2000–2015

- **Pain Point**: Aging copper infrastructure, resident complaints
- **Solution Fit**: Network-as-a-Service (NaaS) — $0 CapEx retrofit
- **Messaging Frame**: "Quote the switch" — replacing failing infrastructure at no capital cost

### Secondary Target: Class A / Post-2015

- **Pain Point**: Need for smart building readiness, IoT, amenity-wide roaming
- **Solution Fit**: Managed WiFi — premium resident experience
- **Messaging Frame**: Future-proofing, resident retention, amenity differentiation

## Product Mapping

| Product | Target Class | Buyer Focus | Key Persona Group |
|---------|-------------|-------------|-------------------|
| NaaS | Class B (2000–2015) | Property management, IT/Ops stakeholders | Property Management + IT/VP Ops |
| Managed WiFi | Class A (Post-2015) | Developers, asset managers | Development + Asset Management |

## Qualification Criteria

A qualified Elauwit target meets:
- [ ] Privately held multifamily owner-operator or developer
- [ ] 1,000–20,000 total units in portfolio
- [ ] Has properties with 200+ units
- [ ] Class B with aging infra OR Class A needing smart building
- [ ] Geographic presence in focus markets (bonus, not required)
