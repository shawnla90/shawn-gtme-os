# Exol (GreenBox) Buyer Personas

## 3 Buyer Archetypes

### Strategic Sam — The Supply Chain Architect

- **Typical titles**: VP Supply Chain, Director Supply Chain, VP Logistics, Director Logistics, Head of Supply Chain, VP Fulfillment, Director of Fulfillment, Director of Cold Chain Ops
- **Focus**: Reducing cost-per-order, improving OTIF performance, eliminating manual errors
- **Messaging**: Cost efficiency, compliance, automation ROI
- **Pain language**: "We're getting killed on OTIF chargebacks" / "Our error rates spike every peak season"

### Ops Olivia — The Ecommerce Executor

- **Typical titles**: Director of Ecommerce Operations, Head of Ecommerce Operations, VP Ecommerce, Director of DTC Fulfillment, Ecommerce Operations Manager, Shopify Logistics Lead, Director of Omni-Channel Operations
- **Focus**: Customer experience, reducing WISMO tickets, syncing inventory across channels (Shopify, ERPs)
- **Messaging**: Real-time visibility, integration, customer satisfaction
- **Pain language**: "Our WISMO tickets are through the roof" / "Inventory is out of sync between Shopify and our 3PL"

### Scale-Minded Shawn — The Executive Decision Maker

- **Typical titles**: Chief Operating Officer (COO), EVP Operations, SVP Operations, Chief Logistics Officer, SVP Fulfillment & Logistics
- **Focus**: Profitability, risk reduction, scaling without heavy CapEx
- **Messaging**: Capital efficiency, risk mitigation, strategic scale
- **Pain language**: "We can't justify $100M for a warehouse we might outgrow" / "Labor is our biggest risk"

## Query Usage Notes

- **Strategic Sam** is the primary outreach target — most likely to champion internally
- **Ops Olivia** responds best to tech-stack-friction messaging (Shopify, Gorgias integration pain)
- **Scale-Minded Shawn** is the budget authority — use for executive-level outreach only
- Cross-reference with revenue ($50M–$500M for mid-market) before enriching contacts
- "Shopify Logistics Lead" and "Ecommerce Operations Manager" are mid-level but high-signal for DTC brands
