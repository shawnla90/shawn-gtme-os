# Attack Angles

## Angle 1: "Your sales team deserves better tools"

**Target:** Companies with SDRs doing manual outreach.
**Pain:** Low reply rates, no personalization, wasted time on research.
**Hook:** Show them what their outreach could look like with a personalized page backing every email.

## Angle 2: "The page is the pitch"

**Target:** Companies relying on slide decks and generic emails.
**Hook:** Build their page live - for THEIR company. Let the deliverable do the selling.
**This is the strongest angle.** The page itself is the demo of what we build.

## Angle 3: "Own your pipeline"

**Target:** Companies paying agencies or consultants.
**Pain:** Renting someone else's process. Data leaves when the contract ends.
**Hook:** We build yours. You own the pipeline, the data, the pages, the sequences. Nothing leaves with us.

## Angle 4: "Scale without headcount"

**Target:** Companies that can't afford to hire RevOps yet.
**Pain:** Need ops but can't justify the hire.
**Hook:** The pipeline IS the ops team. Automated research, enrichment, page generation, CRM sync, outbound - no new headcount.
