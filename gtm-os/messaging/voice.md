## Key GTM Voice Notes

- Substance over polish. Show the work, don't describe it.
- No em dashes in customer-facing copy. Use " - " (spaced hyphen).
- Lowercase energy. We're builders, not marketers.
- Every claim needs proof: specific example, implementation detail, results, or gotcha.
- Direct. Say what we do, not what we could do.
- No "leverage," "unlock," "empower," "seamlessly," "cutting-edge."
