# Objection → Angle Map

How to counter each objection with the right angle.

| Objection | Best Angle | Why |
|-----------|-----------|-----|
| "We already have a CRM" | Angle 1 (better tools) | CRM is step 1, we're steps 2-7. We make their CRM better. |
| "How is this different from a lead gen agency?" | Angle 3 (own your pipeline) | Ownership is the differentiator. |
| "We're too small for ABM" | Angle 4 (scale without headcount) | ABM is focus, not size. |
| "I can do it myself" | Angle 2 (page is the pitch) | Demo the thing they can't build quickly. Show their page. |
| "What's the ROI?" | Angle 3 (own your pipeline) | Full attribution, they see everything in PostHog + Attio. |
| "We need to think about it" | Angle 2 (page is the pitch) | Page is already live. 30-day TTL creates natural urgency. |

For full objection responses, see `demand/objections.md`.
