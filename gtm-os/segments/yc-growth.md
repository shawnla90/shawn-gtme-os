# Segment: YC Growth

## Who

Y Combinator alumni companies. Series A-B. 30-200 employees. B2B focus with GTM/sales function.

## Why They Buy

- Post-YC, need to scale beyond founder-led sales
- Investors expect rapid pipeline growth
- YC network gives them awareness but not infrastructure
- Need to operationalize outbound before Series B board meeting

## Where to Find Them

- Y Combinator company directory (ycombinator.com/companies)
- Exa semantic search (YC + funding + B2B)
- Crunchbase YC alumni filter
- YC Demo Day press coverage

## Pain Points

- Graduated from YC but still doing founder-led sales
- First VP Sales hire needs pipeline infrastructure yesterday
- Spending on 5+ point tools but no ops layer connecting them
- Low reply rates on cold outreach (generic, no personalization at scale)

## Decision Maker

- CEO/Founder (most common at this stage)
- VP Sales / Head of Sales (if hired)
- Head of Growth / RevOps

## Budget

$5K-$25K/mo for GTM infrastructure and tooling

## Buying Trigger

Series A closed, need to show traction. New sales leader hired. Board meeting in 60-90 days demanding pipeline metrics.
