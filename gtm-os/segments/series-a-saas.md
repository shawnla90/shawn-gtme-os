# Segment: Series A SaaS

## Who

B2B SaaS companies. Series A (recently raised or about to raise B). 30-200 employees.

## Why They Buy

- Investor pressure to show revenue growth
- Building first GTM function from scratch
- Need to prove unit economics before Series B
- Can't afford enterprise tools or enterprise headcount

## Where to Find Them

- Crunchbase funding alerts
- Exa semantic search (funding + SaaS + sales)
- Y Combinator alumni (post-Series A)
- TechCrunch / tech press coverage

## Pain Points

- Founder-led sales hitting ceiling
- First sales hire doesn't know how to build pipeline infrastructure
- Spending on point tools but no ops to connect them
- Low reply rates on cold outreach (generic emails)

## Decision Maker

- CEO/Founder (if still doing sales)
- VP Sales (if hired)
- Head of Growth

## Budget

$5K-$20K/mo for GTM infrastructure

## Buying Trigger

Board meeting in 2-3 months. New VP Sales hire needs pipeline fast. Founder wants to stop doing sales calls.
