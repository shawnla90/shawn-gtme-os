# Signal: Job Postings

Company is hiring for sales/RevOps/GTM roles.

## Why It Matters

- Indicates growth phase and budget for tools
- New hires need pipeline infrastructure fast
- Pain points are fresh and top of mind

## What to Look For

- VP Sales, Head of Revenue, Director RevOps
- SDR/BDR team hiring (scaling outbound)
- Sales Enablement roles (need tooling)
- RevOps or Sales Ops (building the function)

## How to Monitor

Exa job posting queries per target company. `research.py` includes job postings in its 4 deep-dive queries.
