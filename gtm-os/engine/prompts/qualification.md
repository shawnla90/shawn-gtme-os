# ICP Qualification Scoring Prompt

Scores accounts on ICP fit (0-100).

## Positive Signals (increase score)
- B2B SaaS product
- Series A through D funding
- 50-500 employees
- Has a sales team (SDRs, AEs, sales leadership)
- Revenue operations or sales ops function
- Recent hiring in GTM roles

## Negative Signals (decrease score)
- Enterprise 10K+ employees
- Nonprofit
- Consulting or agency
- Pre-revenue / pre-product
- Government
- B2C
- Already using full enterprise GTM stack (Outreach + Salesforce + 6sense)

## Output

Single integer score 0-100 with brief justification.
