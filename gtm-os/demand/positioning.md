# Positioning

## Core

"We don't sell software. We build your GTM machine and run it."

## vs Agencies

Agencies give you a playbook and a monthly retainer. We give you infrastructure that compounds. Every campaign makes the next one better because the data stays yours.

## vs DIY

You could hire 3 people and spend 6 months wiring together 8 tools. Or we hand you a running system in weeks.

## vs Enterprise Tools (Outreach, 6sense, Demandbase)

Those are built for 500+ person orgs with dedicated ops teams. We're built for the 50-person SaaS that needs enterprise-grade GTM without the enterprise headcount.

## Key Differentiator

The landing page IS the pitch. Not a slide deck, not a cold email with nothing behind it. A custom-built page showing exactly what we'd do for them - specific stats, specific challenges, specific deliverables, their brand colors, their timeline. They see the work before they talk to us.
