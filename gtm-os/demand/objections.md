# Objection Handling

## "We already have a CRM"

Great. We're not replacing it. We feed it qualified, researched accounts with pages already built. Your CRM gets better data. We integrate with Attio, HubSpot, or whatever you run.

## "How is this different from a lead gen agency?"

Agencies give you leads. We give you infrastructure. When we're done, you own the pipeline, the data, the pages, the sequences. Nothing leaves with us.

## "We're too small for ABM"

ABM isn't about size, it's about focus. If you have 50 target accounts you really want to close, this is more efficient than spraying 10,000 emails. The whole point is doing more with less.

## "I can do this with Apollo + Lemlist myself"

You can send emails, sure. Can you generate a custom landing page for each prospect that shows you researched them specifically? That's the difference between cold email and a warm introduction.

## "What's the ROI?"

Track it yourself. PostHog on every page, Attio tracks every stage. You'll see exactly which accounts viewed their page, which replied, which converted. Full attribution from first touch to closed deal.

## "We need to think about it"

The page we built for you has a 30-day TTL. It's live now. Take a look, share it with your team. If it's not useful, it depersonalizes automatically. No pressure, no follow-up unless you want it.
