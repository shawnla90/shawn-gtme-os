# Ideal Customer Profile

## Target

B2B SaaS companies, Series A through D.

| Attribute | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| Business model | B2B SaaS |
| Funding | Series A through D |
| Employees | 50-500 |
| Sales team | Yes - SDRs, AEs, or sales leadership |
| RevOps/Sales Ops | Exists or being built |
| Geography | US-first, English-speaking markets |

## Hard Constraints (Disqualifiers)

- Enterprise 10K+ employees
- Nonprofit
- Consulting / agency
- Pre-revenue / pre-product
- Government

## Positive Signals

- Hiring for sales/RevOps roles
- Recent funding (last 6 months)
- Using CRM but no sales engagement tool
- Publishing content about scaling sales
- Founder still doing sales (ceiling approaching)

## Negative Signals

- Already using full enterprise GTM stack (Outreach + Salesforce + 6sense)
- No sales team at all
- B2C business model
- Consulting/services revenue (not SaaS)
