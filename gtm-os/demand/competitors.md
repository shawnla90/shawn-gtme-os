# Competitive Landscape

## Direct

Other ABM agencies/consultants doing similar work. Small, fragmented market. Nobody is doing full-stack automated pipeline with personalized pages as the pitch.

## Platform

| Platform | What They Do | Why We're Different |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| Outreach/Salesloft | Email sequencing | We do sequencing + research + page gen + CRM - the full stack |
| Apollo | Contact database | We use Apollo as a component, not the whole solution |
| 6sense/Demandbase | Intent data + ABM | Enterprise pricing, enterprise complexity. We're for 50-person teams. |
| ZoomInfo | Contact/company data | Data source, not an operations engine |
| Clay | Enrichment waterfalls | Enrichment layer only - no outbound, no pages, no CRM sync |

## DIY

Internal RevOps teams building their own stack. They can - it takes 6 months and 3 people. We compress that.

## Key Insight

Most competition is "do nothing" or "hire more people." The automated personalized-page-as-pitch approach is unique. Nobody else generates a custom landing page per prospect as the opening move.
