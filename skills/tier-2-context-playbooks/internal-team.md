# Internal Team Playbook

> Inherits from: `tier-1-voice-dna/core-voice.md`

## Context

Slack, Notion, internal docs.

## Tone

Operational, clear, low ego.

## Format

- What was done
- What's live
- What's blocked
- What's next

## Avoid

- Walls of text
- Vague updates
- Asking open-ended questions without context
