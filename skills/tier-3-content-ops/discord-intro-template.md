## Discord Style Rules
- all lowercase
- no apostrophes (im, its, dont)
- no quotes ever
- short paragraphs with line breaks
- genuine questions > statements
- one emoji max
- link only if natural (not required)

## What Makes It Work
- humble background story
- specific tools/achievements (not vague)
- gaming/cultural references that filter for your people
- ends with engaging question
- authentic voice throughout
